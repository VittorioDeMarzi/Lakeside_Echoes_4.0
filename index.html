Lake Echoes is back under the open sky
and the fourth chapter of our comeback lands
with another heavy-hitting lineup.

The fourth chapter of our story lands with another heavy-hitting lineup - but above all, with the same spirit that brought us together from day one.

Lakeside Echoes 4.0 is not just another open air - it’s a gathering of friends, old and new, by the calm waters of Karpfenteich, 12435 Berlin. Born as a playful experiment among a close-knit crew, this party has grown organically, always keeping its heart in the right place: the music, the people, and the shared moments.

Join us on July 19th, 2025, from 15:00 until late for a day of deep grooves, raw techno, and lakeside connection. Powered by a carefully tuned sound system and surrounded by nature, we invite you to dance, recharge, and lose track of time together.

Expect a local and international lineup bringing hypnotic beats, minimal textures, and sunrise energy - crafted for open-minded dancers and sonic explorers.

A bar will be available on site with community-friendly prices, but feel free to bring your own drinks if you prefer.

Bring your best mood, and good energy. Cold drinks, shaded corners, and a crystal-clear lake await.

Let’s echo through the forest once more.

What is Lakeside Echoes?

Lakeside Echoes started as a spontaneous experiment - a playful idea from a group of friends who simply wanted to gather all their people in one unforgettable open air. The first two editions took place on a rooftop, where the project found its initial energy and identity. As the vision grew, so did the need for a deeper connection with nature - leading us to move the gathering lakeside.

What began as a one-off celebration has evolved organically, edition after edition. Now at its fourth chapter, we're still doing it with the same DIY spirit - growing together, refining the experience, and shaping a space where music, nature, and connection meet in harmony.
